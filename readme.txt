### GoHigh SEO – WordPress AI SEO Plugin to Rank Higher 📈 ###
★★★★★

**GoHigh SEO** helps you stay ahead of competitors with **AI-powered SEO tools** built for modern search engines, AI assistants, and evolving search experiences.

The plugin brings **advanced AI SEO tools directly into WordPress** and assists you with optimizing content, generating Schema Markup, intelligent internal linking, keyword optimization, SEO analysis, and so much more.

**If you always feel you can do more on the SEO** front for your website but don't have the time, then **GoHigh SEO is what you're looking for.**

== Features ==

= Free Features =

* **Simple Setup Wizard** – GoHigh SEO practically configures itself with a step-by-step installation and configuration wizard that sets up SEO for your WordPress site perfectly.
* **Rich Snippets / Schema** – With support for 16+ types of Schema Markups, you'll be able to optimize your posts for rich snippets in just a few clicks.
* **Unlimited Focus Keywords** – GoHigh SEO lets you optimize your posts for unlimited focus keywords per post.
* **Content AI** – Built-in AI for creating SEO-friendly content.
* **XML Sitemap** – Automatically generate an XML sitemap to help search engines index your site.
* **Redirections** – Easily set up 301, 302, and 307 redirections.
* **404 Monitor** – Track and fix 404 errors on your site.
* **WooCommerce SEO** – Optimizing your store products is easier with GoHigh SEO.
* **Elementor SEO** – Deep integration with Elementor.
* **Divi SEO** – Seamless integration with Divi.
* **Local SEO** – Add Local Business Schema Markup to your site.
* **Google Search Console** – Connect and view your search performance data.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `gohigh-seo` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** menu in WordPress.
3. Complete the Setup Wizard that appears after activation.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to create an account? =

No account is required. GoHigh SEO works as a fully standalone plugin.

= Is this compatible with WooCommerce? =

Yes! GoHigh SEO includes deep WooCommerce integration with product schema, GTIN support, and more.

= Will this conflict with other SEO plugins? =

It is recommended to deactivate any other SEO plugins before activating GoHigh SEO to avoid conflicts.
